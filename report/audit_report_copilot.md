## Top 3 Priorities (Do These First)

### 🔴 Priority 1: Implement Comprehensive Schema Markup
**Impact:** Very High | **Effort:** High | **Timeline:** 2 weeks

**Why it matters:** Schema markup tells search engines (traditional and AI) exactly what your content is about. Currently, Google, Perplexity, and ChatGPT are *guessing*.

**What to implement:**
- **Organization schema** on homepage: Declare EigenAI as an entity with logo, address, phone, social profiles
- **Article schema** on all case studies: Mark them as articles with author, publication date, headline, description
- **Author schema** on blog posts & case studies: Link to "yibin" and "unni" author profiles with bios
- **FAQ schema** on FAQ section: Structure each Q&A pair for featured snippets

**Example impact:** With Article schema, your case studies become eligible for AI search engines' "cite this" feature. Perplexity users searching "predictive maintenance in shipping" will see your MISC Marine case study as a source. That's visibility.

---

### 🔴 Priority 2: Create About Page with Team Credentials
**Impact:** Very High | **Effort:** Medium | **Timeline:** 1 week

**Why it matters:** GEO is fundamentally about E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness). Your case studies prove you have expertise, but *who* runs EigenAI? Unknown.

**What to add:**
- Founder/co-founder bios: Name, role, background (e.g., "10 years in data science")
- Key team members: Data scientists, engineers, strategists with LinkedIn links
- Credentials: Relevant certifications (AWS, Google Cloud, ML certifications, etc.)
- Press/recognitions: Any awards, media mentions, speaking engagements
- Company story: How EigenAI started, mission, why you specialize in AI consulting

**Example:** "Yibin leads data strategy with 12 years in enterprise ML at [Company]. She holds AWS Data Analytics certification." That single sentence transforms your credibility for AI engines.

---

### 🟠 Priority 3: Optimize for Voice Search (Question-Based Headings + Schema)
**Impact:** High | **Effort:** Medium | **Timeline:** 2-3 weeks

**Why it matters:** Voice search and AI assistants parse question-answer patterns. Your FAQ hints at this but your blog/case studies don't.

**What to do:**
1. **Reformat H2s in blog & case studies** to use question format:
   - Current: "Propensity Modeling Benefits"
   - Better: "How Does Propensity Modeling Improve Marketing ROI?"
   
2. **Add Speakable schema** to key answer sections (first 1-2 paragraphs after each question heading)

3. **Expand FAQ section** with 10-15 industry-specific questions organized by category:
   
   **AI Readiness & Strategy:**
   - "How do I know if my company is ready for AI?"
   - "What does an AI strategy consultation look like?"
   - "How do you identify high-impact AI use cases?"
   - "What's the typical ROI timeline for AI projects?"
   
   **Technical & Implementation:**
   - "What's the difference between ML and generative AI?"
   - "How long does an AI implementation typically take?"
   - "Do you handle data engineering or just ML models?"
   - "Can you integrate AI into our existing systems?"
   
   **Industry-Specific (Shipping, Ecommerce, Supply Chain):**
   - "How can AI improve supply chain efficiency?" (link to supply chain case study)
   - "What's predictive maintenance and why does it matter?" (link to shipping case study)
   - "How do predictive models reduce customer churn in ecommerce?" (link to furniture case study)
   - "How do I measure AI ROI in my specific industry?"

**Example payoff:** A user asks Alexa or Google Assistant "How does predictive maintenance work?" Your answer becomes a candidate for voice search results.

### Schema Markup (JSON-LD Format)

All schema should be added via JSON-LD in the `<head>` of each page. Examples:

**Organization Schema (Homepage):**
```json
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "Organization",
  "name": "EigenAI",
  "url": "https://eigenai.co",
  "logo": "https://eigenai.co/logo.png",
  "description": "AI consulting and data science services for enterprise transformation",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "streetAddress": "32 Pekin St, #05-01",
    "addressLocality": "Singapore",
    "postalCode": "048762",
    "addressCountry": "SG"
  },
  "telephone": "+65 96343448",
  "email": "info@eigenai.co",
  "sameAs": [
    "https://www.linkedin.com/company/eigenaico/"
  ]
}
```
